Shih Tzu

The name translates to "Little Lion" in Mandarin.

  • Canis lupus familiaris
  • Weight: 9 to 16 pounds.
  • Height: 8 to 11 inches.
  • Lifespan: 12 to 16 years.
  • Color: Various, including black, white, gold, blue, and silver.

The Shih Tzu is a small, affectionate toy breed weighing 9–16 pounds, originally bred as a companion animal for Chinese nobility. Known for their long, luxurious double coat and affectionate, lively, and friendly temperament, they are excellent family pets that thrive on human companionship. They require regular grooming and moderate exercise, adapting well to apartment living
